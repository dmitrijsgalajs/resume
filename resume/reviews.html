Reviews

MDN main focus is at Web development therefore more accurate information may be found on their web page, all of the resources they provide are free to use.

Lynda.com is a greate resource and you may find anything about everything and more. Web tutorials are clear and have online tests to review how well you understood the learnt material.

There are many more resources to look at, such as W3C, YouTube with many chanels dedicated for learning aspects of Web developement and many more. But we will focus on Mozilla Developer Network and Lynda.com

  • Mozilla Developer Network

    MDN provides you with online tutorials that are easy to find and read. It doesn't matter if you are just starting to learn or if you have a career in web developement, everyone may find something useful here.

    Learn HTML module - for the beginners

    Examples

    You may find great examples, some of which are interactive.

    Mozilla Hacks

    As an example Flexbox inspector was designed by Victoria Wang as a part of Firefox DevTools and is a very useful tool to understand Flexbox layout model as well as when you are pushing the boundries of Flexbox layuout

    Browser Compatibility

    Every example on MDN website is provided with a browser compatibility reference and is a helpful thing to decide whether you may or may not use some elemnts and design approches.
